De Paul School

De Paul School, Berhampur, is named after St. Vincent De Paul, one of the pioneers of social reformation. The school had a humble beginning with 56 children and over the years it grew in size and popularity and has the strength of over 2800 students today. Dedicated service of the teachers under an able administration and perfect management has paved the way for the success of this institution.

St Vincent School

St. Vincent Convent School, Brahmapur, Ganjam , Odisha 760001, was founded in january 1954 by the Daughter of Charity, of St. Vincent DePaul. The Management was transferred to Mother M. Gemma, B.S. a Catholic religious Sister and Superior General of the Congregation of the sisters of Little Flower of Bethany, Mangalore on May 10,1979 to cater to the educational needs of the locality.

St Xaviers School

St. Xavier's High School, Ambapua Berhampur, is a Senior Secondary School (XI-XII), affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). The School is a Coed Day School, with classes from Pre KG to XII. It is an English Medium school. The school is located in Ambapua area of Berhampur. St. Xavier's High School, Ambapua was established in 1963.One of the best schools of the city.
